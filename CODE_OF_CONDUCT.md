# Maverick Code Of Conduct

## Expected Behavior

Be respectful, specific, and patient. Critique ideas and code, not people.
Respect privacy: do not publish another person's identity, location,
infrastructure, accounts, private messages, credentials, or logs.

## Unacceptable Behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, deliberate intimidation, unwanted sexual
attention, doxxing, and encouraging abuse are not accepted. Do not use project
spaces to enable malware, credential theft, intrusion, scanning, spam, or
illegal activity.

## Reporting And Enforcement

For ordinary conduct problems, open a short issue without private details and
ask the maintainer to coordinate privately. For a security-sensitive report,
follow `SECURITY.md`. Maintainers may edit or remove content and may warn,
temporarily restrict, or permanently exclude participants when needed to keep
the project safe.

This policy applies in project repositories, issue trackers, review threads,
and project-representative communication.
